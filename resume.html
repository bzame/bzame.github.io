Undergraduate Thesis

Title
AMonteCarloStudyofthePropertiesofaNon-ParametricEstimator for the Area Under the ROC-Curve
Supervisor
prof. Monica Chiogna
Description
Area Under ROC Curve non-parametric estimator properties study via Monte Carlo simulation. The estimator is based on the K nearest neighbor method. This thesis purpose is estimator unbiasedness validation and estimator variance approximation. With this work, my supervisor and I have shown the proposed non-parametric estimator is unbiased and normally distributed. We also proposed a bootstrap approach to estimate its variance.
